Personal Profile

I have worked at aquarium stores for over 3 years. I have H. W. Heir Memorial Scolarship for my work with planted aquariums. I am now the operations manager at Parrots of the World and an enthusiastic hobbiest.

Salt-Water

I have a 16 gallon biocube. This tank contains corals from all over the world, multiple fish whom occupy different niche within the tank and stunning, invertabrates. This equipment is meticulously maintained to provide both a beautiful display and a healthy enviorment for the creatures within.

Planted Tanks

My specialty and pasion are planted aquariums. Ive set up numerous tanks, both in peoples homes and my own. I develop complex ecosystems that are incredibley stable, requireing minimal inputs from the outside world.

Ponds

Ponds are both the easiest and most rewarding aspects of my hobby. I am able to correctly set up fully self sustaining ecosystems. I feed only once or twice a month. The majority of the fish nutrition comes from insects and algae that are naturally forming. in addition these creatures breed and multiply creating stunning specimans.
